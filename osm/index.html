Kayla began helping contribute data to OpenStreetMap around December 2017. OpenStreetMap is a completely free streetmap and an alternative to the proprietary Google Maps. She began by exploring the map and tinkering with a few editing tools. There was a massive need for additions and changes to the map of York County, Pennsylvania. She made a todo list of areas to work on and has been checking off items ever since.
