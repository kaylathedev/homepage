While in high school, Kayla made an Android app called the York Fire Finder. She used Eclipse but later migrated over to Android Studio for developing the app. York Fire Finder was a helpul app for residents of York County to view fire department calls. It showed the same information that would be shown on a firefighter's pager. She later had to discontinue the app due to licensing issues. This app was published on the Google Play Store and had free and pro versions. The pro version offered more features.
